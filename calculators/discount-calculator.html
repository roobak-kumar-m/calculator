How the calculation works

The discount amount is the original price multiplied by the discount percentage, and the final price is what remains.

Final Price = Original Price - (Original Price × Discount %)

When to use this calculator

Use it while shopping, comparing promo codes, or checking whether a sale price is actually worth it.

FAQ

What is the discount amount?

It is the amount taken off the original price based on the discount percentage.

Does this work for sale tax pricing?

Yes. You can calculate the discounted price first and add tax afterward if needed.

Advertisement
Can I use decimal percentages?

Yes. The discount field accepts decimal values like 12.5 percent.

Is money saved the same as discount amount?

Yes. The saved amount equals the discount amount when there are no extra fees.

Can I compare two offers?

Yes. Run the calculator twice to compare sale prices and find the better offer.
