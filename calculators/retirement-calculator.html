How the calculation works

The calculator compounds your current savings and monthly contributions at the selected annual return rate over the remaining years.

Corpus = Future Value of Savings + Future Value of Monthly Contributions

FAQ

What is the retirement corpus?

It is the total projected amount you may have when you reach retirement age.

Does the calculator account for inflation?

No. It projects nominal growth and does not reduce the result for inflation.

Advertisement
Can I change monthly contributions later?

Yes. Adjust the contribution value to see how changes affect your future corpus.

What return rate should I use?

Use a realistic long-term return estimate based on the type of investments you plan to hold.

Is this a guaranteed result?

No. Investment returns vary, so treat the result as a planning estimate only.
