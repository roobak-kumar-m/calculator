FAQ

How do I use the tip slider?

Move the slider to choose a tip percentage between 0 and 30 percent.

Does this split the bill evenly?

Yes. The calculator divides the total bill by the number of people you enter.

Advertisement
Can I use it for delivery or takeout?

Yes. You can use it for any bill where you want to add a tip or gratuity.

Does it include taxes?

No. Enter the bill amount you want to tip on, including tax if you prefer.

Can I set zero tip?

Yes. The slider includes zero percent for situations where no tip is needed.
