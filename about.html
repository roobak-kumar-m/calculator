About Calcbi

Calcbi is a free financial tools platform built to help everyday people — and businesses — make smarter money decisions without needing a finance degree.

Our Mission

At Calcbi, we believe financial literacy should be available to everyone, not just those who can afford an accountant or financial advisor. Our goal is to take the intimidation out of financial math and give every person — whether you are a college student on a tight budget, a first-time homebuyer comparing mortgage offers, or someone planning for retirement — the tools to make confident decisions with their own money. We build calculators that are genuinely accurate and simple enough to use without any special knowledge.

What We Offer

We provide a growing library of free, browser-based calculators built around real financial problems people face every day. Our current tools include:

  • Loan & Mortgage Tools: Understand your monthly EMI, how much total interest you will pay, and how your loan gradually gets paid off over time.
  • Savings & Investment: See how your money can grow using our Compound Interest and Retirement calculators.
  • Everyday Math: Quickly figure out a sale price, calculate a fair tip at a restaurant, or solve any percentage problem in seconds.
  • Global Finance: Convert between major world currencies, whether you are traveling or running an international business.
  • Tax Estimation: Get a clearer, more realistic picture of what your paycheck actually looks like after taxes with our Salary Tax Estimator.

Why Choose Calcbi?

There is no shortage of financial tools on the internet, but a lot of them are cluttered, slow, or buried behind a login wall. Calcbi is different. Every tool on this site is completely free, works instantly in your browser, requires no account, and collects none of your personal or financial data. We also believe in transparency — we show you the formula and walk through real examples so you understand how the numbers work, not just what they are.

Who We Are

Calcbi is run by a small team of developers and finance enthusiasts who wanted to build a cleaner, faster, and more educational alternative to the tools they kept finding online. We are constantly working to improve existing calculators, add new ones, and refine the experience based on real user feedback. If you have a suggestion or spot an error, we genuinely want to hear from you.
