Privacy Policy

This page explains clearly how Calcbi handles advertising, analytics, and the limited information involved in running the site.

Last updated: May 2026

At Calcbi (accessible from calcbi.tech), the privacy of our visitors is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and what we do — and do not do — with it.

If you have any questions about this policy, or want more details about how your data is handled, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Information We Do Not Collect

Calcbi is designed to be a private, secure utility. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, postal address, or phone number. More importantly, all financial data you enter into our calculators — such as your salary, loan amount, or savings balance — is processed entirely within your own web browser. That data is never sent to, saved on, or accessed by our servers.

Google AdSense and Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google and its advertising partners use cookies to show ads that are relevant to you based on your previous visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's advertising cookies allow it and its partners to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history across the internet.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Our advertising partners may use cookies and web beacons on our pages. These third-party ad servers use technology embedded in their ads and links to send content directly to your browser. When this happens, they automatically receive your IP address. Other technologies such as cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons may also be used by these ad networks to measure campaign effectiveness and personalize the ads you see. Calcbi does not have access to, or control over, the cookies used by third-party advertisers.

Calcbi has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

Log Files and Google Analytics

Like most websites, Calcbi uses server log files. These logs record basic information when you visit the site, including your IP address, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time of your visit, referring and exit pages, and the number of clicks. This information is not linked to anything that could personally identify you. It is used purely for analyzing site trends, improving performance, and understanding general visitor behavior. We use Google Analytics for this purpose.

Your Privacy Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

GDPR Data Protection Rights: If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights over your personal data. We will take reasonable steps to allow you to access, correct, update, or request deletion of any personal data we may hold about you (for example, an email address submitted through our contact form). To make such a request, please contact us directly.

CCPA Privacy Rights: If you are a California resident, you have the right to request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal data we have collected about you. You also have the right to request that this data be deleted from our systems.

Consent

By continuing to use our website, you agree to this Privacy Policy and its terms and conditions.
